This page contains links to resources that are not on the web and/or are not free that mention TSK and/or Autopsy.  Resources on the web can be found in [Help Documents](/Help-Documents/) or [Autopsy User's Guide](/Autopsy-Users-Guide/). 

# Books
(in alphabetical order)
* [Computer Forensik 2nd Edition](http://computer-forensik.org/). Alexander Geschonneck. German Language. 2006. dpunkt.
* [Digital Evidence and Computer Crime: Forensic Science, Computers, and the Internet 2nd Edition](http://www.corpus-delicti.com/fs_bookstore/decc/). Eoghan Casey. 2004. Elsevier.
* [File System Forensic Analysis](http://www.digital-evidence.org/fsfa/). Brian Carrier. 2005. Addison Wesley.
* [Forensic Discovery](http://www.aw-bc.com/catalog/academic/product/0,1144,020163497X,00.html). Dan Farmer and Wietse Venema. 2005. Addison Wesley.
* [Incident Response and Computer Forensics 2nd Edition](http://books.mcgraw-hill.com/getbook.php?isbn=007222696X). Mandia, Prosise, Pepe. 2003. Osborne.
* [Intrusion Detection und Prevention mit Snort & Co.](http://www.os-t.de/buecher_new.php). Ralf Spenneberg. German Language. 2006. Addison Wesley.
* [Know Your Enemy 2nd Edition](http://www.honeynet.org/book/index.html). The Honeynet Project (Brian Carrier wrote the UNIX Forensics chapter and Rob Lee wrote the Windows Forensics chapter). 2004. Addison Wesley.
* [Malware: Fighting Malicious Code](http://www.phptr.com/bookstore/product.asp?isbn=0131014056&rl=1). Ed Skoudis with Lenny Zeltser. 2004. Prentice Hall.
* [Real Digital Forensics](http://www.realdigitalforensics.com/). Keith Jones, Richard Bejtlich, and Curtis Rose. 2005. Addison Wesley.
* [Security Warrior](http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/swarrior/index.html). Cyrus Peikari and Anton Chuvakin. 2004. O'Reilly.
