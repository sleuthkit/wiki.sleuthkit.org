## Big Picture
This section contains links to articles on using The Sleuth Kit as a whole (i.e. the articles are not about a specific tool). 
* [Law Enforcement and Forensic Examiner Introduction to Linux: A Beginner's Guide](http://www.linuxleo.com/). Barry Grundy. 2003 (NASA OIG) - Updated December 2008.
* [CyberGuardians Cheat Sheet](http://www.cyberguardians.org/docs/ForensicsSheet.pdf): 2-page PDF with sample commands for a variety of common TSK activities.  Useful for veteran analysts who want to quickly look up a forgotten flag, or for a beginner looking to learn by example and experimentation.
* [Running Sleuthkit and Autopsy Under Windows](http://www.memophage.net/Running_Sleuthkit_and_Autopsy_Under_Windows.pdf)  by Charles Lucas ([local copy](http://www.sleuthkit.org/sleuthkit/docs/lucas_cygwin_v1.2.pdf))
