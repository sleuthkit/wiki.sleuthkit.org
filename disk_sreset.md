## disk_sreset
disk_sreset was a Linux-only tool that could remove a host Protected Area (HPA) from a hard disk. The HPA was  temporarily  removed so that the full disk can be acquired.  When the disk is powered off or reset, then the HPA will exist again.

This tool was removed from TSK.  Use hdparam instead.
