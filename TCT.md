TCT is short for The Coroner's Toolkit, which was developed by Wietse Venema and Dan Farmer. [TSK](/The-Sleuth-Kit/) branched from TCT many years ago and supports many more file systems and platforms. It can found at <http://www.porcupine.org/forensics/tct.html>(http://www.porcupine.org/forensics/tct.html).
